# ASK FOR HELP

### Post your help request in #ask-for-help-here:
1. Share a brief message about what is happening.
2. Attach your latest log file:
  - Enter the game, press `F1` to open the console.
  - Click `Open logs folder` (top-right), then drag the correct log file into Discord.
3. A screenshot can help:
  - Use `PrtSc` or your OS screenshot tool, then `paste` into Discord chat.
  - If preferred, DMs to AGF are open for private help.
