## V1 Locked Scope (Primary And Only Edits For Now)

Only the following needs are in scope for v1 edits:

1. Auger and chainsaw noises.
2. Impact noises related to harvesting and surface interaction.
3. Gun fire.
4. Explosions.
5. Vehicles (excluding horn sounds).
6. Electrical and block device looping noises (for example generators and block/device loops), with turret firing included under gun fire.
7. UI noises for completed crafting items.
8. Spider zombie noise volume.
9. Animal hurt/pain/death sounds (including dog-related sounds).
10. Placing blocks, upgrading blocks, and repairing blocks (regardless of tool).

### V1 Scope Rules

- If a sound is not tied to one of the 10 items above, it is out of scope for v1.
- Category-level adjustments are preferred first; per-sound overrides are for specific outliers.
- Turret loop/mechanical audio belongs with electrical/block loops.
- Turret firing audio belongs with gun fire.

### Mapping File Status

- docs/sounds-categorized.tsv now includes:
  - V1Scope (Primary or OutOfScope)
  - V1PrimaryNeeds (one or more of the 10 scope tags)
